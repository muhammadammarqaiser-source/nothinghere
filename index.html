Our History

Our History

Journey through the milestones that have shaped Career Institute Jhang

2005: Foundation

Career Institute Jhang was established with a vision to provide quality career education to the youth of Jhang.

2008: First Expansion

Added new programs in IT and Business Administration due to increasing demand.

2012: Campus Upgrade

Moved to a larger campus with state-of-the-art facilities and computer labs.

2015: Industry Partnerships

Established partnerships with leading companies for internships and job placements.

2020: Digital Transformation

Launched online learning platform to continue education during pandemic.

2025: Present Day

Over 5000 students trained, with 85% successful placement rate in various industries.
